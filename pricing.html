Questions About Pricing?

We keep things simple and honest. Here are the most common questions we get. Still not sure? Just talk to us — no pressure.

Let's Talk
What's the difference between monthly and 1-time payment? +
Monthly payment means we handle everything for you on an ongoing basis — your website stays updated, SEO keeps growing, and we manage your ads every month. The 1-time payment is a done-for-you setup: we build everything out, hand it over to you, and you own it forever. Monthly is better for long-term growth. 1-time is great if you want the foundation built fast and manage it yourself.
Are there any hidden fees? +
No hidden fees, ever. What you see is what you pay. If you run Google Ads, your ad spend goes directly to Google — that's separate from our management fee, and we'll always be upfront about what you need to budget.
Do I need to sign a long-term contract? +
Nope. Monthly plans are month-to-month — you can cancel anytime. We don't believe in locking you in. We keep clients because they see results, not because of a contract.
Is there a setup fee? +
Monthly plans include setup at no extra charge. We get everything built and ready in the first 2 weeks, then your monthly billing kicks in. 1-time payments are all-inclusive — you pay once, we build everything.
Can I upgrade my plan later? +
Yes, anytime. Most clients start with Starter or Growth, see results, and upgrade to Scale when they're ready. We make the switch easy and seamless.
How soon will I start getting more bookings? +
SEO takes 60–90 days to gain momentum. Most clients on the Growth or Scale plan see a real increase in calls and form submissions within 30 days of launching.
